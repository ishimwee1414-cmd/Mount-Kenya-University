Welcome to Mount Kigali
University


Empowering generations through Quality Education


Mount Kigali University is a leading citadel of teaching, research and academic
excellence at the heart of Kigali in Rwanda. The University started as a Campus
of Mount Kenya University in 2010 and has since grown into a fully-fledged
university accredited by the Rwanda Higher Education Council under
the Ministry of Education. Today, the Board of Directors of MKUR have invested in
excess of 10 billion Rwandan Francs on establishing post-modern infrastructure
including well equipped laboratories, administration offices, teaching and
learning facilities, a library, hostels, and recreational facilities.


The strategic foundation of MKUR is embedded in delivery of quality education
in pursuit of realizing competent and employable graduates. It is our pride that
throughout the Republic of Rwanda you are likely to find our alumni in high
profile positions both in the private and public sectors. We have a growing
international student population from Kenya, South Sudan, Tanzania, Cameroon,
Uganda, Burundi, Gabon, Democratic Republic of Congo, Nigeria, Chad, Asia,
Middle East and U.S.A
